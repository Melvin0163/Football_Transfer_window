Official Rule Book

MANAGER INBOX

Objective & Guidelines

  • Teams act as football club managers to build the strongest squad possible.
  • Represent one global powerhouse club from Europe's Top 5 Leagues.
  • A player can belong to only one club and be transferred only once throughout the event.
  • All transfers must be officially recorded. No negative budget allowed.

Transfer Window

  • Teams can trade or buy players from each other within strict budget constraints.
  • Exchange deals (straight swap or swap + cash) are fully permitted to secure targets.
  • Verbal agreements mean nothing. Once a deal is logged by officials, it is locked.

Sealed-Bid Auction

  • Organizers announce unchosen superstar players from global leagues.
  • Teams submit secret (sealed) bids. No one knows what rivals are spending.
  • Total bids across multiple active players cannot exceed total remaining budget.
  • Highest bidder secures the player. Ties are resolved by an official coin-toss.
